Marcio Morais

I've been an illustrator since 1989, working with various merchandising brands such as Toey, Disney, Marvel, DC Comics, Unesco, Sega, Viacom, McDonalds and others. Since then I have also worked as UX Design Leader for Brazilian banks (Bradesco, B3, Banco Paulista) and as Product Manager for Claro (telecommunications) and Luizalabs/Magazine Luiza (e-commerce). I also have 4 IT books and 3 child books (with characters developed by me) published. Visit my portfolio page to know more about my illustration works or my Linkedin account to know more about my career as Product Manager/UX Lead.
